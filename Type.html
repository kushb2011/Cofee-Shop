Home Products How To Make Coffee Types Of Coffee

Black

Black coffee is just ground coffee beans steeped in hot water, served warm. No cream. sugar or syrups.

Latte

As the most popular coffee drink of them all, the latte is made of a shot of espresso and steamed milk with just a touch of foam.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a latte made with more foam than steamed milk and sometimes topped off with cocoa or cinnamon powder

Americano

Americano has a similar flavor to black coffee as it consists of an espresso shot diluted in hot water.

Espresso

Espresso is a small, concentrated shot of coffee that can only be made with an espresso machine.

Macchiato

The macchiato is another espresso-based drink that has a small amount of foam on top, so a stronger cappuccino

Mocha

The mocha is a chocolate espresso drink with steamed milk and foam.

Iced Coffee

A coffee with ice, typically served with a dash of milk, cream or sweetener

Cold Brew

Cold brew coffee is made by steeping coffee beans from anywhere between 6-36 hours and served with cold milk or creamer.
